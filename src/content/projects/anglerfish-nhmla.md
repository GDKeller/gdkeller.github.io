<p>Designed, built and installed an Arduino-driven light sensor system for the display of a rare Pacific footballfish specimen at the <a target="_blank" href="https://nhm.org/anglerfish">Natural History Museum of Los Angeles</a>. The fish's tissue is highly sensitive to light, so the ichthyology collection staff needed a way to limit and track light exposure.</p>
